The Character

Jayce, Brillant Inventor

Milestone Event

After Victor's proposal, refused by Jayce, where they would use a crystal that would allow humans to become one with technology. Victor took the crystal. The officials refused to help Jayce to avoid a potential military retaliation between the two cities (Piltover and Zaun). He then decided to take care of it himself, returned to his laboratory, and began preparations for an attack. A period of research follows, and he finally creates his masterpiece: The Mercury Hammer.With it, Jayce went to Zaun. He manages to get rid of Victor's acolytes, who are powered by the arcane crystal. He understands that the crystal must be destroyed, but Victor struggles. Jayce manages to break the crystal.Exhausted but victorious, he returned home and was received as a hero. With this adventure, Jayce soon realized that his actions had attracted the attention of dangerous enemies. But Jayce is determined and motivated to defend his people. Jayce is now Piltover's best chance for a bright future.
