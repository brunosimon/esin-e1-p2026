During the “Arcane” Series, the character of Singed is not central but is nevertheless dealt with superficially to complement Viktor's story. He is also present as a doctor for Silco, which explains his presence during the explosion caused by Powder during episode 3. He finds himself trapped in the flames of the explosion and comes out disfigured. Later in the series we find him in his basement, basement in which he will meet Viktor as a child, alongside a strange creature, the result of experiments by chemist Singed.

Over the course of the series, he plays a number of important roles, being the originator of the drugs running in Zaun but also the main actor in Jinx's rescue.

Several theory comes to play around this strange person, since for connoisseurs of League Of Legends lore, we know that he is at the origin of the birth of Warwick. The hottest theory today is that Vinder could turn into Warwick after his injection of Shimmer. Also from League Of Legends stories, we know that death was a crucial part of the creation of Warwick, a death suffered by Vinder. Heavy suspicion can be attributed to this theory, but what do you think?
